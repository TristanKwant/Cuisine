What we remember most strongly about it was the distinct lemongrass flavor and the softly cooked aubergine that nearly melted on our tongues. plaatje: vegan-apple-tartlets.jpg serves: 4 time: 10 min difficulty: easy tag1: vegan tag2: main meal tag3: asian found-title: The green kitchen Travels found-by: Jamie Oliver found-img: found-link: item1: 2 courgettes item2: 150 g runner beans item3: 70 g ciabatta item4: 6 radishes , with tops item5: 3 cloves of garlic item6: 100 ml white wine , or organic vegetable stock item7: olive oil item9: 25 g Cheddar cheese item10: extra virgin olive oi item11: 1 teaspoon runny honey item12: 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar item13: a few sprigs of fresh dill, flat-leaf parsley, chives step1: Preheat the oven to 190ºC/375ºF/gas 5. step2: Trim the courgettes, halve lengthways, then slice, along with the runner beans, into 2cm pieces. Tear the ciabatta into 2cm chunks and add all this to a roasting tray, along with the radishes and unpeeled garlic. step3: Pour in the wine or stock, season with sea salt and pepper and drizzle over 2 tablespoons of olive oil. step4: Grate the Cheddar and sprinkle over the bread pieces. Roast in the oven for 35 to 40 minutes, until the courgettes are cooked through. step5: Combine 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil with the honey and vinegar. Pick and finely chop the herbs and stir through; season and set aside. step6: Remove the tray from the oven and drizzle with the dressing. This is lovely served on a bed of spinach. ---
